Noggin 🧠✨

AI-Powered Adaptive Learning

Learning that adapts to you.

Noggin is an adaptive educational platform combining game-based learning and neuroplasticity to empower children with autism, ADHD, dyslexia, and other brain disorders to thrive in Math, English, Science, and beyond.

🚀 Features
Built for the way your child learns. Every feature is designed with neurodivergent learners in mind, combining cutting-edge science with joyful experiences.

Game-Based Learning: Fun, interactive challenges that build cognitive skills.

AI-Powered Adaptation: Difficulty, pace, and style adjust to each child’s profile.

Progress Tracking: Analytics for parents and educators.

Safe & Inclusive: Secure, distraction-free environment with accessibility at its core.

Parent & Teacher Tools: Collaboration features to keep everyone in sync.

📚 Subjects
A full curriculum, reimagined for neurodivergent learners:

Mathematics: From counting to algebra with visual, tactile experiences.

English: Reading, writing, and phonics with multi-sensory approaches.

Science: Hands-on experiments and explorations.

Social Studies: Geography, history, and culture through interactive storytelling.
